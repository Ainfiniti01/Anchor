# Anchor ⚓

**A Memory-Powered AI Accountability Companion**

Anchor is an AI-powered accountability companion designed to help individuals manage unhealthy habits through personalized support, self-reflection, progress tracking, and adaptive guidance.

Unlike traditional habit trackers, Anchor remembers important aspects of a user's journey, learns what motivates them, identifies behavioral patterns, and provides context-aware support over time.

---

## Problem

Many people struggling with unhealthy habits lack consistent accountability and support.

Existing solutions are often:

* Generic and one-size-fits-all
* Expensive or inaccessible
* Focused only on tracking rather than understanding
* Unable to provide support when users need it most

Users frequently lose motivation because their goals, progress, triggers, and personal reasons for change are forgotten.

Anchor was created to solve this problem.

---

## Solution

Anchor acts as an AI accountability companion that remains available throughout the user's journey.

The system combines:

* Personalized AI conversations
* Long-term memory
* Progress tracking
* Behavioral pattern recognition
* Reflection and accountability tools

to help users stay connected to their goals and make better decisions over time.

---

## Core Features

### 🧠 Memory System

Anchor remembers meaningful information shared by the user, including:

* Goals
* Future ambitions
* Motivations
* Personal values
* Triggers
* Important milestones

The AI uses this information to provide more personalized and relevant responses.

---

### 💬 AI Accountability Companion

Users can choose how the AI interacts with them:

#### Supportive Friend

Warm, encouraging, conversational support.

#### Neutral Companion

Balanced and objective guidance.

#### Accountability Coach

More direct and goal-focused accountability.

The AI adapts its responses based on the selected style.

---

### 📈 Progress Tracking

Anchor tracks:

* Current streak
* Best streak
* Recovery streak
* Daily urges
* Resisted urges
* Relapse history

Users can visualize progress through weekly charts and behavioral insights.

---

### 🔍 Behavioral Insights

Anchor analyzes behavior patterns and provides insights such as:

* High-risk days
* Frequent triggers
* Urge trends
* Recovery improvements

Insights are only shown when sufficient data exists.

---

### 🧩 Reflection System

Rather than simply giving advice, Anchor encourages self-awareness through:

* Reflection prompts
* Identity-based reminders
* Goal reconnects
* Pattern recognition

The focus is helping users understand themselves better.

---

### 🔐 Privacy & Security

Anchor includes an optional security layer:

* PIN protection
* Auto-lock system
* Failed-attempt protection
* Local privacy controls

Users can protect their progress and conversations from unauthorized access.

---

* Urge frequency

to identify opportunities for support and intervention.

---

### Progress Engine

Anchor calculates:

* Current streak
* Best streak
* Recovery streak

## Design Principles

Anchor is built around several core principles:

### Accuracy Before Encouragement

The AI should never invent progress or assume improvement.

### Support Without Judgment

Users receive guidance without shame, guilt, or criticism.

### Personalization Through Memory

Conversations improve over time by remembering what matters.

### Reflection Over Manipulation

Anchor helps users see patterns and reconnect with their values rather than using pressure or fear.

### Privacy First

Users remain in control of their data and security settings.

Anchor is an accountability and self-reflection companion.

It is not a replacement for professional medical, psychological, or addiction treatment services.
